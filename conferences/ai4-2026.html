Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ai4 have a hotel room block?

Yes — Ai4 negotiates a discounted rate at The Venetian available until July 9, 2026 or until it sells out. Check ai4.io/venue for the block link. If the block rate is competitive, book through there. If Expedia is cheaper, book Expedia.

Why is August the worst month for outdoor commuting?

104°F average high with direct desert sun and no shade. A 10-minute outdoor walk feels like 20 minutes and you'll arrive at sessions visibly uncomfortable. The Venetian Expo's location mid-Strip means rideshare is relatively cheap — but at $15 each way twice a day for 4 days, that's $120 in Ubers you didn't budget for.

When should I arrive and depart?

Ai4 officially recommends arriving Monday August 3 morning. Sessions start Tuesday August 4. Depart Friday August 7 or later. The conference goes through Thursday August 6 evening.

Ready to Book?

Each hotel link goes directly to that property's Expedia page with August 3–7 dates pre-filled. The Venetian and Palazzo fill up fast for Ai4 — the conference room block sells out before the conference date every year.
