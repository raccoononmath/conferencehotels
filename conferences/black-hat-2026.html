🔥 August heat warning: Las Vegas averages 104°F in August. Mandalay Bay is at the south end of the Strip. Stay connected or budget $15+ per Uber ride. The free tram to Luxor/Excalibur is your best friend for affordable options.
🎩 Staying for DEF CON? DEF CON 34 runs August 6–9 at the Las Vegas Convention Center (mid-Strip). If you're doing both, book July 31 – August 10. You'll need to relocate mid-week or rideshare between Mandalay Bay and the LVCC (~$15–20 each way). Some attendees book Mandalay Bay for Black Hat then switch to a mid-Strip hotel for DEF CON.
🚃 Free tram: Connects Mandalay Bay → Luxor → Excalibur. Runs until ~10:30pm. If you stay at Luxor or Excalibur you save money and still have a zero-sweat commute to Briefings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Black Hat Trainings and Briefings?

Trainings run August 1–4 — 2 or 4-day intensive technical courses, sold separately and often expensive. Briefings are August 5–6 — the main conference with keynotes, research talks, Arsenal tool demos, and the Business Hall. Most attendees come for Briefings. Some do both.

Can I stay for both Black Hat and DEF CON?

Yes and many people do. DEF CON runs August 6–9 at the Las Vegas Convention Center. The LVCC is mid-Strip — about 3 miles from Mandalay Bay. If you want one hotel for both, Excalibur or MGM Grand are reasonable midpoints, though neither is ideal for both venues simultaneously. The smarter play is Mandalay Bay for Black Hat then a short Uber to DEF CON daily.

How early should I book for Black Hat?

Black Hat's own hotel block at Mandalay Bay sells out months in advance. Check blackhat.com for official block rates when registration opens. If you miss the block, Expedia prices in August Las Vegas are still manageable — book at least 3–4 months out.
