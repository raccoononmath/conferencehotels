🎩 Coming from Black Hat? Black Hat runs August 1–6 at Mandalay Bay — DEF CON follows immediately at the LVCC. Many attendees do both in one trip. If so, consider switching hotels mid-week or booking a mid-Strip hotel that works for both venues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DEF CON cash only?

Yes — DEF CON badges are purchased on-site in cash only. Credit cards are not accepted for badge purchases. ATMs at the LVCC and nearby casinos are your friend. Badge price is typically $400–500.

Can I do Black Hat and DEF CON in the same trip?

Yes. Black Hat runs August 1–6 at Mandalay Bay (south Strip). DEF CON runs August 6–9 at LVCC (mid-Strip). The overlap day is August 6. Many attendees commute between venues on that day or switch hotels mid-week.

Does DEF CON have official hotel blocks?

Yes. DEF CON typically negotiates rates at nearby properties. Check defcon.org for block information. The Fontainebleau has been a recent DEF CON partner hotel.
