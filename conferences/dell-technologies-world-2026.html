Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Dell Technologies World 2026?

The Venetian Expo Center, 201 Sands Ave, Las Vegas NV 89169. The expo is physically connected to The Venetian Resort and The Palazzo.

When should I arrive?

Sessions start Monday May 18 morning. Arrive Sunday May 17. Most attendees leave Thursday May 21 evening or Friday May 22 morning.

Is there a Dell hotel room block?

Dell typically offers a negotiated room block at The Venetian. Check the official conference registration site for current block pricing — it often matches or beats Expedia rates for the on-site hotel specifically.

How hot is Las Vegas in May?

Late May averages around 95°F (35°C) in the afternoon. Mornings are pleasant. Mid-day outdoor walks are hot but manageable — unlike June/July. Still worth staying connected or nearby to avoid sweating through your shirt before morning keynotes.

Ready to Book?
