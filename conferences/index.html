Why These Guides Exist

Expedia and Booking.com show you hotels sorted by price or rating. They don't tell you that the hotel that looks "1 mile from the venue" is actually a 30-minute walk through casino floors in 100°F heat — or that there's a free tram that makes a budget hotel perfectly viable.

Every guide on this site is built with the actual venue location, real transport options (free trams, monorails, AWS shuttles), and direct booking links that go to that specific hotel's page with conference dates pre-filled. No generic city searches. No fake hotel IDs.
