🏔️ November SLC weather: Cold — average highs around 47°F (8°C), with freezing nights. Pack a proper coat. The mountains are stunning in November and skiing at nearby resorts (Park City, Snowbird) starts around now.

🚊 TRAX light rail: Salt Lake City has a good light rail system (TRAX) that runs downtown and to the airport. The Convention Center TRAX station is steps from the Salt Palace. A day pass is $5.50 — useful for getting around without a car.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is KubeCon 2026 really in Salt Lake City?

Yes — the official CNCF/Linux Foundation site confirms KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America 2026 is November 9–12 in Salt Lake City, Utah at the Salt Palace Convention Center.

How do I get to Salt Lake City?

SLC (Salt Lake City International Airport) is 8 miles from downtown. TRAX light rail runs from the airport to downtown in about 25 minutes for $2.50. Rideshare takes 15–20 minutes.

Is there skiing near KubeCon in November?

Yes — Park City, Snowbird, and Alta are 30–45 minutes from downtown SLC. November is early season; snowpack varies, but resorts typically open mid-November. If you're staying an extra day, checking lift status is worth it.
