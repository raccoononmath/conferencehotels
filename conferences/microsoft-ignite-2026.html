Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Microsoft Ignite 2026?

Moscone Center, 747 Howard St, San Francisco, CA 94103. November 17–20, 2026.

How many people attend Microsoft Ignite?

Around 20,000 in-person attendees, with 200,000+ digital attendees. Smaller than Dreamforce but still significant enough to impact nearby hotel availability.

Is Microsoft Ignite the same as Microsoft Build?

No. Microsoft Build is the developer-focused conference (typically in Seattle or online in May). Ignite is the IT professional/enterprise conference, larger in scale, focused on Azure, M365, and security.
