Frequently Asked Questions

Why do people stay at The Venetian for Money20/20?

Money20/20 is extremely meeting-heavy — C-suite density means your schedule is often back-to-back 1:1s. Staying on-site means 3-minute walks between meetings instead of rideshares. At $15+ per Uber, 4 days of commuting adds up fast.

When should I book for Money20/20?

Book 3–4 months before the event. The Venetian block (when available) should be your first call. Expedia pricing for October in Las Vegas is reasonable compared to summer conferences.

What is October weather like in Las Vegas?

October is one of the best times to visit Las Vegas — highs around 78–82°F (26–28°C), warm but not oppressive. Evening walks on the Strip are comfortable. Far better than the August heat for conferences.
